# Faith Aiya
Applied Economist
Gainesville, Florida. faith.aiya@ufl.edu

I am a 4th-year PhD student in the Department of Food and Resource Economics at the University of Florida. My areas of specialization includes agricultural marketing, consumer behavior and experimental economics. 
Specifically, I use discrete choice and neuro experiments to examine consumer preferences and the underlying decision-making mechanisms that shape them, and I use my findings to generate actionable, policy-relevant insights into food choices and consumer behavior for key stakeholders.

## Research Interests

- Consumer preferences and food choice
- Agricultural marketing and branding
- Fairness in food systems
- Discrete choice experiments
- PLS-SEM and behavioral research methods

## Current Projects

- Consumer responses to fairness labels in fresh produce markets
- State image and state-sponsored agricultural marketing programs
- Food lifestyle changes among U.S. shoppers
